WELCOME TO
GAME VIEW LODGE

Game View Lodge is an up-market Lodge situated in Vryburg next to the Leon Taljaard Game Park. Whilst enjoying a sundowner on our deck you will be able to view game which includes 2 of the big 5 and the most beautiful sunsets.

Vryburg, also known as the Texas of Africa, is situated in the North West Province on the main route to Namibia and Botswana. Thus being the ideal place to stay over. Come and experience comfort!

Our Story

A DREAM COME TRUE!!!

Theo Volschenk, ex-Head of Health Department, and Tersia Volschenk ex Bank Manager decided to quit their jobs and follow their dream to own their own Lodge. After 3 years of planning and building, this dream finally came true.

We would like you to think of Game View Lodge as your home away from home and we are looking forward to treating you and ensuring that you have a comfortable stay and memorable time enjoying our hospitality and also breathtaking sunsets.
